2FA - Two Factor Authentication

With 2FA (2-Step Verification), you can add an extra layer of security to your account. After your first login, from your account, you can enable the 2FA security option.
Enabling 2FA on your account will require on each Sign-In an extra 8-digit code provided by a 2FA compatible mobile application, where the 2FA code will be installed, such as Google Authenticator or FreeOTP. The 2FA option can also be disabled anytime, if you enabled it.
