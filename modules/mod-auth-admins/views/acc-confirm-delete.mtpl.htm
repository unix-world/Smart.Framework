Delete Account


Important:
Deleted accounts cannot be recovered after permanent deletion.

Instead of account permanent deletion a better option is to set that account as INACTIVE for a while.

To PERMANENT DELETE the selected account click the bellow button and:
* select [OK] to permanent delete it
or
* select [Cancel] if you do not intend so.
